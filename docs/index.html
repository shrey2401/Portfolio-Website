About Me

Throughout my college years, I delved into the fundamentals of Data Science, mastering software tools like Microsoft Access and MySQL, while also gaining insights into hardware. Moreover, I'm deeply connected to my spiritual side, actively engaging in practices that allow me to worship and connect with a higher purpose. My portfolio reflects not just my technical skills, but also the breadth of experiences and interests that define me as a well-rounded individual committed to growth and excellence.
